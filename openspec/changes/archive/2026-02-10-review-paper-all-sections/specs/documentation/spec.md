## ADDED Requirements
### Requirement: Paper Review and Revision
The documentation SHALL undergo a systematic review of all paper sections, identifying and fixing errors, logic gaps, style issues, and cross-section inconsistencies.

#### Scenario: Review completed and fixes applied
- **WHEN** the review is completed
- **THEN** all identified issues are documented in the tasks file with categorization
- **THEN** all fixes are applied to the corresponding `docs/paper/sec*.md` files
