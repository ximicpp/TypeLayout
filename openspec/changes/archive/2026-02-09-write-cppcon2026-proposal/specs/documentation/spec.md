## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: CppCon 2026 Session Proposal
The project SHALL have a complete CppCon 2026 session submission document covering title, abstract, outline, target audience, and supplementary materials.

#### Scenario: Submission meets CppCon format requirements
- **WHEN** the proposal is submitted to CppCon 2026
- **THEN** it SHALL include a title, session type, level, abstract, detailed outline with timing, key takeaways, target audience, prerequisites, and speaker bio
- **AND** the abstract SHALL be self-contained and compelling to the review committee

#### Scenario: Outline covers all library capabilities
- **WHEN** the outline is reviewed
- **THEN** it SHALL cover the problem statement, P2996 foundations, two-layer system, real-world applications, formal proofs, and cross-platform toolchain
- **AND** total timing SHALL be 60 minutes including Q&A
