## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Boost Submission Strategy Analysis
The project SHALL maintain a documented analysis of the costs, benefits, and risks of submitting to Boost libraries.

#### Scenario: Developer reviews submission decision
- **WHEN** evaluating whether to pursue Boost submission
- **THEN** a comprehensive cost/benefit analysis is available
- **AND** clear Go/No-Go criteria are defined
- **AND** a phased decision framework with gates is documented

#### Scenario: Strategy includes risk assessment
- **WHEN** reviewing the submission strategy
- **THEN** P2996 standardization risks are quantified
- **AND** review rejection risks are assessed
- **AND** maintenance commitment risks are documented
- **AND** mitigation strategies are provided for each risk

### Requirement: Phased Submission Approach
The submission strategy SHALL follow a phased approach with explicit evaluation gates.

#### Scenario: Gate 0 - Interest email
- **WHEN** initiating Boost community engagement
- **THEN** an [interest] email is sent to gauge community reaction
- **AND** feedback is collected before committing to full preparation

#### Scenario: Gate 1 - P2996 progress check
- **WHEN** evaluating after community feedback
- **THEN** P2996 standardization progress is assessed
- **AND** decision to proceed is based on standardization trajectory

#### Scenario: Gate 2 - Final commitment
- **WHEN** proceeding to formal submission
- **THEN** time and maintenance commitments are explicitly confirmed
- **AND** the decision is documented with rationale
